SDGs Workshop

As an extension to the morning’s conference, Youth & Change and International Youth Council Morocco Chapter held in 23 September 2018 a second workshop session orientated toward sustainable development goals. Participants have been given the opportunity to prepare and come up with their own personal socially-impactful projects, to help and build a society focused on achieving the SDG’s. Because we are the future!

SDGs Conference

On behalf of SDGs anniversary Youth&Change held in 23 September 2018 a conference in partnership with the International Youth Council Morocco Chapter as an empowering move for Moroccan Youth. This Action Campaign presented a great opportunity to inspire and empower young people with knowledge, platforms ,tools and showcasing concret methods for youth orientation in different fields to actively contribute towards the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals. Because we are the future!
